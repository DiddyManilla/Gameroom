How to Play: As the die is rolled, the number of pips on the die adds to the number of points. Click 'stand' after each round to continue playing. Click 'sit' to finalize your points. If two of the same number in a row are rolled, than the players still standing lose all of their points.

How to Play: Mancala is a two player game. In each half of the board is a goal to store points. The players each take turns picking a circle. Each point in the circle is placed one at a time into consecutive circles including your goal, but not your opponent's. If the last point lands in your goal, you go again. A player wins by getting the most points in their goal.

How to Play: Cold War is a two player arms race. Based on the rules of the ancient game Senet, players take turns flipping sticks for a number. A player moves a piece along the winding path. The goal of the game is to get all of your pieces off the board, so you can convert them into nuclear weapons. Beware, though. Two pieces in a row cannot be jumped onto. If your enemy boxes you in, you'll have to surrender by disconnecting.
